### Repeatable tasks

- ev(ery) day (@ 6:00)
- ev(ery) Mon(day) (@ 6:00)
- ev(ery) other day (@ 6:00)
- ev(ery) odd/even day (@ 6:00)
 * 1 January is odd day, 2 January is even 
- ev(ery) 3 days (@ 6:00)
- ev(ery) 25 (@ 6:00)
 * this means every 25th day of the month. If month has no such day this task will not be performed that month
